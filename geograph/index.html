Dengue cases at Taiwan from year 2014 – In Taiwan, there are two types of dengue fever cases, imported case and indigenous case. Imported cases is the cases that “imported” from foreign countries, that patients had traveled to some South-East Asia countries before he/she got sick. The imported cases is then pass the disease to the mosquito (Aedes). Indigenous cases is the cases that the patients did not traveled to those countries with dengue fever. Therefore they got the disease by local areas (was bitten by local mosquito). » dengue, map, plotly

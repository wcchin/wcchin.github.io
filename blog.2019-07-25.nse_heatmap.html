Dynamic Heat Map

A series of heat map showing the density of the students in week 4 Tuesday to Thursday (3days) samples, using different time steps:

  1. density in every 1 minutes: 1 minute heat map
  2. density in every 5 minutes: 5 minute heat map
  3. density in every 10 minutes: 10 minute heat map
  4. density in every 15 minutes: 15 minute heat map
  5. density in every 30 minutes: 30 minute heat map

Change the slide bar at the bottom (right of the time slide bar) from 3fps to 10fps for faster view.
